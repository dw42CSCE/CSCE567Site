Visualization Dashboard

About This Project

This dashboard explores state-level trends in gun ownership, poverty, and homicide rates over time, allowing viewers to compare states, identify potential correlations, and reflect on patterns that emerge across decades. Users can explore line charts, color-coded maps, and area plots, all of which update interactively based on year selection and state filtering.

Use the top navigation or scroll to view the dashboard. All visualizations are hosted via Tableau Public and embedded directly in this site for easy access without additional libraries or downloads.
